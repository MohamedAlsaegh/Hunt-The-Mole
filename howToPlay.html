Hunt The Moal

Game Idea

Hunt The Mole is a fast-paced arcade game where players must hit a mischievous mole that randomly pops up from different holes.
Each successful hit earns points, while missed attempts chip away at the player's hearts.

How To Play

  • Click on the mole as it appears to score points.
  • Each miss costs one heart.
  • The game ends when all hearts are lost.
  • Press the Reset button at any time to start fresh with full hearts and zero score.

Score

  • Every successful mole hit increases your score.
  • The score continues to accumulate until the game ends or is reset.

Heart

You start with 3 hearts. Each miss costs 1. Lose them all, game over. Hit reset to restore hearts and score.
